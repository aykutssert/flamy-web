Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Flamy is built to be simple, focused, and motivating. No subscriptions, no noise — just your habits.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Build momentum with daily streaks. Milestone banners at 7, 14, and 30 days keep you going when it counts.

📋

Habit Catalog

Choose from 20+ curated habits across health, focus, mind, movement, and sleep — or build your own.

📊

Progress Charts

Weekly summaries, 4-week bar charts, and a completion heatmap show exactly how far you've come.

🔔

Daily Reminders

Set a daily notification at your preferred time so your habits stay top of mind — even on busy days.

🎯

Milestone Badges

Earn badges at key milestones and track your best streaks on each habit's detail screen.

🔒

Fully Private

All data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics. Your habits are yours alone.

🔒

Your data never leaves your device.

Flamy uses SwiftData to store everything locally. No accounts required, no servers, no tracking. What you track is private — always.
